  2. SFO Runway Usage Analysis
    • Aggregated data from Kaggle and SFO statistics regarding the runways usage at SFO into a Rstudio database.
    • Applied time-series analysis to identify peak hours and trends to generate regression models to study runway usage frequency.
    • Created a forest model with decision trees with a 92% accuracy to determine that runway 28L/R has the highest frequency
    • Identified runway 28L/R has the greatest impact on residential areas, San Bruno, Millbrae, & South SF, within a 12km radius
